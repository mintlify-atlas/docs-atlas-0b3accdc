The AI Chatbot is your personal mental wellness companion, designed to provide a safe space for reflection, conversation, and emotional support. The chatbot uses artificial intelligence to engage with you in meaningful conversations about your mental health and daily experiences.

## Overview

The chat feature allows you to:
- Have private conversations with an AI assistant trained in mental wellness support
- Create multiple chat sessions for different topics or time periods
- Review past conversations at any time
- Get thoughtful, personalized responses to your thoughts and questions

<Note>
The AI chatbot is designed to provide support and reflection, but it is not a replacement for professional mental health care. If you're experiencing a crisis, please reach out to a qualified mental health professional.
</Note>

## Starting a New Chat

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Chat Page">
    Open the side menu and select the Chat option. You'll see a greeting with your name when no chat is selected.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Type Your First Message">
    In the message input field at the bottom, type your question or share what's on your mind. The placeholder text says "Pregunta lo que quieras" (Ask whatever you want).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send and Create Chat">
    Click the send button (arrow icon). The system will automatically create a new chat with a title based on the current date and your first message.
    
    <Info>
    Chat titles are automatically generated using the format: `[Date] - [First 50 characters of your message]`
    </Info>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for AI Response">
    After sending your message, you'll see a loading indicator ("...") while the AI processes your message and generates a thoughtful response. This typically takes a few seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sending Messages

Once you're in an active chat:

1. **Type your message** in the text area at the bottom of the screen
2. **The text area expands** automatically as you type longer messages
3. **Click the send button** (arrow icon) or press enter to send
4. **Your message appears immediately** in the conversation thread
5. **The AI responds** within a few seconds with personalized feedback

<Tip>
The send button is disabled while the AI is generating a response to prevent sending multiple messages at once. Wait for the AI to finish responding before sending your next message.
</Tip>

## Managing Your Chats

### Viewing Chat History

All your chats are stored and can be accessed from the side menu:

- **Chat list**: Shows all your previous conversations with their titles
- **Select a chat**: Click on any chat in the list to view the full conversation history
- **Automatic updates**: Messages are automatically refreshed every 2 seconds to show new AI responses

### Deleting Chats

You can delete any chat from your history:

1. Find the chat in the side menu chat list
2. Click the delete option for that chat
3. The chat and all its messages will be permanently removed

<Note>
Deleting a chat is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you want to remove the conversation before confirming deletion.
</Note>

## Understanding the Chat Interface

### Message Types

The chat displays two types of messages:

- **Your messages**: Appear on one side of the conversation, clearly marked as from you
- **AI messages**: Appear on the opposite side with an AI avatar icon, showing the assistant's responses

### Real-time Updates

The chat interface includes several features for a smooth experience:

- **Auto-scroll**: The chat automatically scrolls to show the latest message
- **Polling**: New messages are fetched every 2 seconds to keep conversations current
- **Loading indicators**: Visual feedback shows when the AI is thinking and composing a response
- **Visibility optimization**: Polling pauses when you switch to another tab to save resources

## Privacy and Security

Your conversations are private and stored securely:

- Messages are associated with your user account
- Each chat is isolated and accessible only to you
- All communication with the AI service uses secure connections
- Your personal information (name, age, email) is included in requests to personalize AI responses

<Info>
The AI uses your profile information (name, age) to provide more personalized and contextually appropriate responses.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

### Messages Not Sending

If your messages aren't sending:
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure the AI isn't still responding to a previous message (wait for the loading indicator to disappear)
- Try refreshing the page if the issue persists

### Chat Not Loading

If a chat doesn't load:
- Verify you're logged in to your account
- Try selecting a different chat and then returning to the desired one
- Refresh the page to reload your chat history

### AI Taking Too Long

If the AI response is taking longer than expected:
- Wait up to 30 seconds for complex responses
- Check your internet connection
- If the issue persists, try sending your message again in a new chat
