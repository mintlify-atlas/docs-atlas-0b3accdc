# Welcome to CicloVital

**CicloVital** is a digital platform designed to accompany people in caring for their emotional wellbeing and mental health. Built with modern web technologies, CicloVital combines AI-powered support with comprehensive habit tracking to help users maintain and improve their mental wellness.

<Note>
  CicloVital represents an academic and technological initiative committed to innovation, meaningful learning, and the responsible use of artificial intelligence for personal development and self-care.
</Note>

## What is CicloVital?

CicloVital is a comprehensive mental health and wellness tracking platform that provides:

- **Emotional Support**: AI-powered chatbot interactions for emotional guidance and support
- **Daily Habit Tracking**: Monitor key wellness metrics including sleep, exercise, energy, mood, and motivation
- **Visual Insights**: Interactive dashboards and statistics to understand your wellness patterns
- **Record Management**: Calendar-based system to view and manage your daily records

## How It Works

1. **Create an Account**: Sign up and authenticate to start your wellness journey
2. **Log Daily Records**: Track your daily habits including sleep hours, exercise, energy levels, mood, and motivation
3. **Chat with AI**: Interact with the AI chatbot for emotional support and record analysis
4. **Review Statistics**: Analyze your data through comprehensive dashboards with customizable date ranges
5. **Track Progress**: Monitor your wellness trends over time through the calendar and statistics views

## Technology Stack

CicloVital is built with modern, reliable technologies:

- **Frontend**: React 19 with Vite for fast development
- **UI Framework**: Ionic React for cross-platform compatibility
- **Mobile**: Capacitor for native mobile app deployment
- **Charts**: Recharts for data visualization
- **Calendar**: FullCalendar for record management
- **Communication**: WebSocket (STOMP/SockJS) for real-time chat

<Card title="Cross-Platform Compatibility" icon="mobile">
  Thanks to Ionic React and Capacitor, CicloVital works seamlessly on web browsers and can be deployed as a native mobile application on Android devices.
</Card>
